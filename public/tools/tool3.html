Visualize the Torque Profile of Your Valve

This tool derives the full torque profile of your valve — BTO, RTO, ETO, BTC, RTC and ETC — from the base torque stated in your valve manufacturer's datasheet. It does not guess or look up values — you provide the base torque and Kv from your specific valve's catalogue page, and the tool applies industry-standard ratios plus your chosen safety factors to build the complete profile your actuator engineer needs.
